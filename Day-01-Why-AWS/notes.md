#  Day 1 - Why AWS?

## Why AWS?

AWS (Amazon Web Services) is one of the world's leading cloud computing platforms.

Instead of buying and maintaining physical servers, companies can use AWS services on demand and pay only for what they use.

## Why do companies use AWS?

- No need to purchase expensive hardware.
- Pay only for the resources you use.
- Easy to scale applications.
- High availability and reliability.
- Strong security features.
- Global infrastructure.

## Benefits of AWS

- Cost Effective
- Highly Scalable
- Reliable
- Secure
- Fast Deployment
- Global Availability
